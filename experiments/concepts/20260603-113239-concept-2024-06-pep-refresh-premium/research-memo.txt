**Research Memo: Website Redesign Concepts for PEP**

**Objective:**
To gather insights from existing beverage brand websites to inform the redesign of PEP's website, focusing on premium, light, and refreshing protein drinks suitable for social and out-of-home consumption.

**Anchored References:**

1. **Revized (https://www.revized-seltzer.com/):**
   - **Product:** Hard seltzer combining sparkling water, fruit flavors, and alcohol.
   - **Relevant Patterns:**
     - **Hero Section:** A concise tagline paired with product highlights such as calorie count, natural ingredients, and no added sugar.
     - **Flavour Grid:** A visual display of each SKU accompanied by brief sensory descriptions.
     - **Drinking Ritual:** Step-by-step serving suggestions, including glassware, ice, and garnishes, which PEP can adapt to showcase consumption occasions.
     - **About & Founder Story:** Dedicated sections providing brand background and founder narratives.
     - **Navigation:** Clean and direct links to Flavours, Serving, About, and Contact pages.
   - **Considerations for PEP:** Emphasize protein content and suitability for out-of-home venues. Incorporate the unique story of founders Lou & Shannon. Utilize PEP's brand palette of greens and creams, avoiding neon tones associated with seltzers.

2. **drinkpep.com (https://drinkpep.com/):**
   - **Product:** "Platinum Energy Probiotics" – an energy drink focusing on gut health.
   - **Relevant Patterns:**
     - **Benefit Blocks:** Sections highlighting product benefits like probiotics, B vitamins, and electrolytes. PEP can adapt this to emphasize protein content, calorie information, absence of added sugars, and gluten-free attributes.
     - **Product Cards:** Visual representations of flavors with concise descriptions.
     - **Testimonials Carousel:** Rotating customer reviews to build trust.
     - **Email Subscription Band:** A section encouraging visitors to stay connected via email.
     - **Lifestyle Photography:** Images showcasing the product in various settings.
   - **Caution for PEP:** Ensure clear differentiation from this brand due to the name similarity. Avoid replicating their copy, ingredients, founders, or positioning.

**Summary of Adaptable Patterns:**

- **Hero Section:** Concise tagline with key product benefits.
- **Flavor Showcase:** Visual grid with sensory descriptions.
- **Consumption Occasions:** Guidance on serving and suitable settings.
- **Brand Storytelling:** Dedicated sections for brand history and founders.
- **Transparent Nutrition:** Clear display of ingredients and health benefits.
- **Customer Engagement:** Testimonials and subscription options.
- **Clean Design:** Minimalist aesthetics focusing on product quality.

By integrating these insights, PEP can develop a website that effectively communicates its brand values and appeals to its target audience.
