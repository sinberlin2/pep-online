# PEP Social Brand Pack

## Brand Idea

PEP is a premium non alcoholic social drink that belongs at the table when everyone else is ordering something fun. It should feel like golden hour, rooftop drinks, music festivals, late dinners, and stylish bar rituals.

The protein is a quiet proof point. The social feeling is the headline.

## Positioning Line

A vibrant, premium protein drink that earns its place on the table when everyone else is drinking.

## Tagline

Feel good. Have fun.

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Meaning for the design |
| --- | --- |
| Social | Made for groups, bars, dinners, rooftops, festivals |
| Vibrant | Rich fruit colour, strong contrast, high energy |
| Premium | Confident typography, controlled detail, elegant spacing |
| Fun | Playful illustrations and badge shapes, never childish |
| Euphoric | Sparkles, bursts, movement, golden evening warmth |
| Fresh | Fruit, fizz, citrus, garnish cues |

## Logo Direction

The PEP wordmark should be bold, rounded, and instantly readable from a distance. The best direction is a chunky custom display logo with soft curves, strong negative space, and a deep plum colour.

Logo requirements:

| Element | Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Wordmark | Large custom PEP lettering |
| Shape | Rounded geometric, thick, compact |
| Feeling | Confident, playful, premium |
| Main colour | Deep Plum `#3B164F` |
| Avoid | Thin wellness type, gym energy type, childish bubble letters |

## Typography Direction

| Use | Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Logo | Custom rounded display wordmark |
| Headlines | Tall condensed sans, confident and editorial |
| Detail text | Clean condensed sans with generous tracking |
| Badges | Bold condensed uppercase |

Typography should feel like a cocktail menu and festival poster had a very good night out together. It should not feel like nutrition packaging.

## Illustration System

Build illustrations as reusable flat graphic assets with warm texture and slight imperfection.

Core assets:

| Asset | Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Mango slice | Golden orange, rounded, carved grid texture, juicy but graphic |
| Coconut half | White centre, brown shell, premium tropical cue |
| Passionfruit | Deep berry shell, yellow seeds, vibrant centre |
| Citrus wedge | Lime and coral variants for freshness |
| Starburst | Coral, lime, and plum versions for celebration |
| Leaves | Deep green cocktail garnish shapes |
| Ribbon | Deep plum social message banner |
| Umbrella or garnish pick | Small nightlife and mocktail cue |

Avoid cute cartoon fruit. The fruit should feel like a stylish drink garnish system, not kids' juice.

## Can Design

Can format: slim 330ml aluminium can.

Visual hierarchy:

1. Large PEP wordmark.
2. Tagline: Feel good. Have fun.
3. Big tropical fruit composition.
4. Small circular `7g protein` badge.
5. Small `0.0%` or alcohol free cue.
6. Footer text: Sparkling fruit protein drink.

The can should look good in someone's hand at a bar, on a dinner table, or next to cocktails. It should not look like a shaker, supplement, energy drink, or gym drink.

## Badge System

Recommended badge copy:

| Badge | Role |
| --- | --- |

| `7g protein` | Quiet credential, never headline |

## Copy Rules

Voice: playful, confident, celebratory, experiential.

Use phrases like:

| Copy | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Feel good. Have fun. | Main tagline |
| Good times, no alcohol required. | Social proof line |
| Made for the table. | Occasion line |
| A better round. | Bar order line |
| Sparkling fruit protein drink. | Product descriptor |

Avoid:

| Avoid | Why |
| --- | --- |
| gym | Pulls the brand into fitness |
| macros | Too functional |
| workout | Wrong occasion |
| supplement | Kills the social mood |
| recovery | Too clinical or sports led |
| health goals | Preachy and solo |
| energy boost | Energy drink cue |

## Production Notes

The generated visual board is a concept direction. For production, rebuild the logo, fruit icons, badges, and can label as clean vector assets so they can be reused across packaging, website, social posts, and pitch materials.

## Direction 2: Inline Brand Inspired

This refinement takes more inspiration from premium non alcoholic social drink brands while keeping PEP distinct.

| Reference influence | What to borrow | What to avoid |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kin Euphorics | Moody colour, euphoric nightlife energy, editorial confidence | Copying psychedelic or wellness-mood claims |
| Lyre's | Spirit-like label structure, refined medallion, grown-up bar ritual | Looking like a direct spirit imitation |
| Seedlip | Botanical ingredient art, premium restraint, considered negative space | Becoming too quiet or old-fashioned |

Design changes from the first board:

1. Use deeper plum and berry colours as the main world.
2. Add champagne gold for premium label detail.
3. Shift fruit from playful icons to botanical cocktail ingredient illustrations.
4. Use a framed central label with ornamental details.
5. Make the can feel like it belongs next to mocktails, aperitifs, and non alcoholic spirits.
6. Keep the `7g protein` callout small and credential-like.
