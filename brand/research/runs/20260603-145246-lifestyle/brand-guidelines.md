# PEP Brand Guidelines — Lifestyle Upgrade Positioning

## Who is PEP for and when do they drink it?

PEP is designed for people who want to make healthy choices feel like a lifestyle treat—not a chore. Our drink is for:

- Urban professionals, creatives, and wellness enthusiasts who value balance, style, and feeling good in daily life
- Anyone who sees wellness as part of their identity—not an extreme or exclusive pursuit
- People who gather in cafés, workspaces, or on the beach and want a light, refreshing, and healthy drink that fits their vibe
- Moments: Brunch, co-working, park days, post-yoga meetups, or a sunny afternoon pick-me-up

## Approved Adjectives (Brand Personality)
- Refreshing
- Approachable
- Balanced
- Vibrant
- Smooth
- Inclusive
- Modern
- Health-focused
- Authentic
- Playful
- Natural
- Sophisticated

## Rejected Vibes
- Gym-bro, fitness, bodybuilding, or extreme sports
- Clinical, medicinal, or “optimizing” language
- Loud, aggressive, or dark visuals
- Energy shot, pre-workout, or night-time club scenes
- Anything resembling drinkpep.com (US, energy/probiotic, or logo/lookalike)

## Visual & Copy Do’s
- **Minimal, pastel color palette** (use only approved brand colors: green, orange, peach, cream, yellow, white)
- **Modern, elegant typography:** Didot for logo, Montserrat for clean and informative copy, Brittany Signature script for warmth and a personal touch
- **Lifestyle photography:** Sunlit, candid, natural moments in real daily use (e.g. café table, beach blanket, stylish brunch)
- **Copy:** Friendly, confident, never clinical—focused on enjoyment, feeling good, and wellness as a treat

## Visual & Copy Don’ts
- No muscle, gym, or energy drink iconography
- No dark, metallic, or harsh color schemes
- No crowded labels or clinical layouts
- Avoid “high-protein!” shouts, performance claims, or intimidating language
- No references or similarities to drinkpep.com in name, logo, or theme

**In-Line Peers:**
- BLOOM (https://bloomnu.com/products/bloom-pop): Fun, lively, wellness-forward prebiotic branding
- EVER TREE (https://www.everleafdrinks.com/): Minimal, elegant, and natural—premium lifestyle drink
- OLIPOP (https://drinkolipop.com/): Soft, health-forward, friendly visual style
- Poppi (https://drinkpoppi.com/): Vibrant wellness, social moment appeal
- Alive & Well (https://aliveandwellkombucha.com/): Modern, smooth, accessible kombucha
- Recess (https://takearecess.com): Pastel minimalism, playful, lifestyle-driven
- Ole Cocktails (https://olecocktails.com/): Vibrant, festive, modern ready-to-drink—with refined accessibility
- Ish (https://ishspirits.com/): Minimal, sophisticated, inclusive spirit alternatives for lifestyle occasions

**Peer Brands (Other Positionings) — Not for direct emulation:**
- Vitamin/functional focus (HOLY, LMNT, NOCCO, VITAMIN WELL): Too functional or sporty, not lifestyle-driven
- Social/mocktail-centric (Kin Euphorics, Lyre's, Seedlip): Lean too heavy on alcohol replacement or event/nighttime cues

**Anti-References (Do NOT emulate):**
- Gym/fitness RTDs (e.g. Lean Body)
- drinkpep.com (US site; avoid confusion and visual collision)
- Aggressive, masculine energy drinks

## Brand Fit Rules
1. **Must** focus on lifestyle enjoyment and daily wellness—not athletic discipline or performance
2. **Use only approved brand colors and fonts**; no harsh, dark, or metallic looks
3. **Photography** = natural, aspirational moments (never gyms, clinics, or night clubs)
4. **Copy** celebrates feeling good and having fun—not "crushing goals" or optimizing performance
5. **Reject all names, visuals, or wordmarks resembling drinkpep.com or mainstream protein/energy drinks
6. **Never position PEP as a meal replacement, supplement, or quick-fix booster—always a treat

---

**Summary Statement:**
PEP belongs in the hand of anyone who wants to feel good and have fun—while making better wellness choices in style. Think: the protein drink that fits your brunch, not your bench press.
