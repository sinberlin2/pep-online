Create a polished editorial visual identity board for the beverage brand PEP.

Brand: PEP
Flavour: Mango Coconut only
Tagline: Feel good. Have fun.

Overall art direction:
Warm, premium, playful, wellness lifestyle drink. Minimal but fruity. Retro-inspired without looking childish. Use a warm off-white editorial background. The brand should feel like an everyday lifestyle upgrade, not a gym protein drink, not an energy drink, not clinical.

Composition:
Create one clean brand board with clearly separated zones.

1. Upper left:
   Large bold PEP wordmark. The wordmark should be very clear and readable. Chunky custom lettering, premium playful geometry, confident, clean, slightly rounded, adult, not childish. Deep forest green.

2. Right side:
   Hero slim 330ml aluminium can, front-facing with a slight three-quarter angle. Cream can body with deep forest green lower band. The can label should include:
   PEP wordmark
   Feel good. Have fun.
   Mango Coconut
   One 7g protein badge
   One 49 calories badge
   330ml

Important can text restrictions:
Only show these words on the can:
PEP
Feel good. Have fun.
Mango Coconut
7g protein
49 calories
330ml

Do not show the words:
sparkling
naturally flavoured
gluten-free
passionfruit
protein sparkling drink
social serve
alcohol
0.0%

3. Middle left:
   Separate reusable illustration elements, arranged neatly:
   whole mango
   mango slice
   coconut half
   green leaves
   small starburst/sparkle shapes
   small ribbon shape without text
   cocktail garnish shapes

Illustration style:
Flat vector-like illustrations. Warm, imperfect, retro-premium, fruity, festive. Use bold simple shapes, soft organic curves, minimal detail, no photorealism.

4. Right side badge concepts:
   Show separate badge concepts next to the can:
   7g protein
   49 calories
   0.0%
   gluten-free
   social serve

Badge concepts can appear on the board, but only 7g protein and 49 calories may appear on the can.

5. Bottom left:
   Colour palette swatches with readable hex labels:
   Deep forest green #2D6A4F
   Soft leaf green #7BA37F
   Warm peach #FFC49D
   Sunflower yellow #FFD166
   Mango orange #F28C45
   Cream #FFF6E8

6. Bottom middle:
   Typography direction:
   Bold rounded display for logo
   Elegant condensed sans for details
   Confident editorial hierarchy
   Show sample text:
   HEADLINE
   Bold. Playful. Confident.
   SECONDARY
   Clean. Modern. Easy to read.

7. Bottom right:
   Flat label layout preview showing the editable can label design. It should match the hero can and include only:
   PEP
   Feel good. Have fun.

   330ml

Text accuracy rules:
Render PEP very clearly and large.
Keep all other text minimal and readable.
Do not invent extra brand names, websites, ingredients, claims, or slogans.
No misspelled duplicate logos.
No passionfruit.
No sparkling.
No naturally flavoured.
No gluten-free on the can.
No 0.0% on the can.

Output intent:
Beautiful first-pass visual direction sheet that can guide later extraction into reusable logo, fruit icons, label assets, colour palette, badges, and can mockups.
