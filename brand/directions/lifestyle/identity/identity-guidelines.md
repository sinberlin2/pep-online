# PEP Lifestyle Upgrade Visual Identity

## Creative idea
PEP should feel like a light everyday treat with credible wellness built in, not a supplement wearing a lifestyle mask. The visual system is spacious, soft, taste-led, and premium-but-approachable: more café table, sunny fridge shelf, and relaxed afternoon than gym bag or clinical routine.

## Colour world
The palette is built around Fresh Teal, Warm Cream, and Soft Coral. Fresh Teal gives refreshment and recognisability; Warm Cream creates a premium, gentle base; Soft Coral brings flavour, appetite, and warmth. Secondary colours — Apricot Fizz, Mist Blue, and Cucumber Glass — support flavour variety and social content without pushing into childish soda territory.

## Type system
Use Fraunces for expressive display headlines and the PEP wordmark style: soft, premium, and lightly nostalgic without becoming formal. Use Manrope for body copy and product facts so the protein benefit remains credible and easy to read. Use Caveat sparingly for relaxed handwritten lifestyle notes, and Space Grotesk for compact badges and product callouts.

## Layout principles
- Lead with whitespace and one confident focal point.
- Treat protein as clear proof, not the whole personality.
- Keep claims in small, calm badges rather than dense technical grids.
- Use rounded containers, soft arcs, fruit-led accents, and minimal sparkle/bubble motifs.
- Avoid gym cues, metallic finishes, aggressive slants, hard neon, clinical diagrams, and party-first nightlife imagery.

## Packaging direction
The can should read like a lifestyle accessory: warm base, generous central wordmark, soft colour banding, a small graphic mark, and a simple benefit line. Flavour cues should be appetising and elegant, using abstract fruit shapes rather than loud soda illustration.

## Photography direction
Use natural daylight, relaxed domestic or café settings, condensation, ceramic surfaces, linen, ice, fruit slices, and real-life refreshment moments. Scenes should feel unhurried and social in a low-key way, never gym-led, medicinal, nightclub-led, or meal-replacement heavy.
