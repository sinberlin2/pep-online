Lou & Shannon — founders

About us

We’re into sports — we met playing volleyball — and we’re Lou and Shannon. Time and again at cafés and bars, we found ourselves missing nice, healthy drink options. We also struggled to hit our protein goals without something that still felt fun and social.

PEP started from that gap: a light, refreshing drink with a protein boost that you actually want to order when you’re out.

Lou brings UX experience — putting product characteristics in line with what people really want, then testing and designing around that.

Shannon brings entrepreneurship experience and works as an AI engineer, building digital products powered by AI — including how we shape PEP online.
